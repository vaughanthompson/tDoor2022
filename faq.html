TransDiesel Portal Loyalty Programme

We believe loyalty has its rewards.

So, from 3 July 2019 we're introducing a rewards points system. Any invoice amounts generated from ordering through the Portal contribute to your rewards points balance.

These points can be redeemed through the Rewards Shop from a range of merch, team clothing or even an account credit voucher!

From time to time we may also reward you with points from other activity, we're working on that. But for now, points can only be accumulated through completed orders placed through the portal.

 


TransDiesel Loyalty Programme - FAQ

How to I get awarded points and when are they added to my balance?

Points are calculated on all orders placed through the portal and are applied to your balance once the order is processed, goods are dispatched and the order has been completed to an invoiced state.


How to I claim an item from the Rewards Shop?

If you have administration privileges on your Portal login or you are the sole login for your company you will be able to complete loyalty redemptions through the Rewards Shop.


Do I earn points individually?

Points are calculated and added on an ACCOUNT BASIS only.


How do we receive our reward?

We deliver! If you have more than one location loaded against your account you can choose at checkout. Plus if you wish to send to a new location (within NZ) you can add this too.


When do we receive our reward?

As soon as possible once the points spend has been processed. Some items may require 1-2 weeks lead time if stock is limited and to ensure any ordering options / personalisation is completed.


Can I claim more than item from the shop?

Absolutely. Like any other shopping cart you can add as many items as you have points!


Are points able to be spent on items we order from the main Portal?

Redemption of points is applicable to the Rewards Shops only at this point.


I've made a purchase on our account at a branch- can I receive points for this?

We're sorry. The points system is only available with the Portal online ordering service.


My reward arrived in poor condition.

Please contact the the TransDiesel marketing team for any issues regarding the Portal Loyalty Programme and we'll do our best to assist you.


 


TransDiesel Loyalty Programme - Terms & Conditions

Loyalty Reward Points may be redeemed for items currently listed on the customer portal, subject to availability.

Points are not transferrable & can only be accumulated on purchases transacted online through the customer portal.

Revenue from any other purchases made outside of the portal will not apply.

Earned points will be deducted for any products returned.

TransDiesel reserves the right to alter the rewards program as they see necessary.

Your dashboard will show you your current points status and will show a net balance after any redemptions are made.

If you redeem an Account Credit, this will be applied automatically to your customer account, held with TransDiesel.
